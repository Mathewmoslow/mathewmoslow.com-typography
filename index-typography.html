A Book with a Soundtrack

I didn't only write journal entries on my phone, I wrote full songs, poems and put them on verses.

With the help of some very patient audio engineers, AI and a generous sprinkling of autotune, those words were put to music and I even sang in part on a few tracks.

Please enjoy this expression of emotion in another form

01

Feels Like Goodbye

This was the first song that came out of me during the divorce. I was trying to make sense of why we were tearing apart something that didn't feel broken—just wrong-shaped, maybe? Neither of us wanted to lose each other completely, but we both knew we couldn't stay married.

While I'm Breathing

I was scrolling through Instagram and came across a post that absolutely struck me—like being hit sideways, and I guess that's what happened to the guy who originally posted it. I took his words and reimagined them somewhat, then spent about 24 hours straight writing and reworking the music.

ABOUT MATHEW MOSLOW

Mathew Moslow writes from the intersection of healing and honesty, where the messy work of becoming human meets the courage to examine what we'd rather leave unexamined.

01

Beaches of Jamaica

MONTEGO BAY

Growing up gay in Montego Bay taught him early that survival sometimes means performing, but his work now centers on the radical act of showing up authentically—especially when it's uncomfortable.

02

Psychiatric Nursing

HOSPITAL FLOORS

A psychiatric nurse in training and a seeker by nature, Mathew's journey from the beaches of Jamaica to the hospital floors of Florida reads like a masterclass in intentional transformation.

03

The Pressure Cooker Method

A REVOLUTIONARY APPROACH

His approach to life's most difficult moments is refreshingly unconventional. When his marriage to Aaron began to unravel, rather than following the traditional script of bitter divorce proceedings, they chose what Mathew calls a "pressure cooker" method—confronting pain head-on, with therapeutic support, to transform heartbreak into something resembling wisdom.

Author & Truth-Teller
